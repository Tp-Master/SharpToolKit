Resize Image Online

SharpToolKit allows you to resize images online quickly and easily. You can change the width and height of your photos directly in your browser without installing any apps. This tool is optimized for mobile users so you can resize images on your phone in seconds.

Resizing images is useful when uploading photos to websites, social media platforms, or documents that require specific image sizes. Large images can slow down websites and take longer to upload. By resizing your image, you can make the file more efficient while still keeping good visual quality.









How to Resize an Image Online

Follow these simple steps to resize your image using SharpToolKit:

  • Select the image you want to resize.
  • Enter the desired width and height in pixels.
  • Click the resize button.
  • Download the resized image.

This process happens directly inside your browser which means your images remain private and are not uploaded to any server.

Best Image Sizes for Social Media

Different platforms require different image sizes. Here are some commonly used dimensions:

  • Instagram Post: 1080 × 1080
  • Instagram Story: 1080 × 1920
  • Facebook Cover: 820 × 312
  • YouTube Thumbnail: 1280 × 720
  • WhatsApp Image: 800 × 800

Using the correct dimensions ensures your images look sharp and professional on social media platforms.
