JPG vs PNG – What's the Difference?

JPG and PNG are two popular image formats used on the internet.

  • JPG – Smaller file size, ideal for photos.
  • PNG – Higher quality, supports transparent backgrounds.

If you are working with graphics, icons, or logos, PNG is usually the better option.

When Should You Use PNG Images?

PNG images are commonly used for:

  • Website logos
  • Graphic design
  • Transparent backgrounds
  • High-quality graphics
